Pilau

A Kenyan cuisine made out of rice, beef and various spices such as pilau masala, tangawizi and garlic.

Ingredients

  • Tangawizi
  • Garlic
  • 1kg Rice
  • Salt
  • Cooking Oil
  • Coriander
  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Chilli

Steps

  1. Put the meat in a sufuria to boil till it is tender.
  2. Remove the meat from the sufuria and add onions along with enough cooking oil.
  3. Let the onions cool till they are golden brown.
  4. Add garlic and tangawizi paste and stir till it is uniformly distributed.
  5. Add rice, salt, pilau masala till satisfaction.
  6. Fill the sufuria with water and let the mixture cook.
  7. Using tomatoes and the left over onions, make kachumbari.
  8. Serve the pilau while hot with a side of kachumbari.
