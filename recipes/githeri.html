Githeri

This is a meal made out of maize and beans. A Kenyan cuisine that is loved by many around the country. Best served with avocado.

Ingredients

  • Maize (of any size depending on the number of people)
  • Beans (of any size depending on the number of people)
  • 2-3 Onions
  • Salt
  • Cooking Oil
  • Coriander

Steps

  1. Mix the uncooked beans and maize together in one sufuria.
  2. Fill the sufuria with water till the mixture has been covered.
  3. Let the mixture cook till the mixture is evenly cooked.
  4. Add cooking oil, salt and onions and leave them to cook for around 30 mins to 1hr.
  5. Serve while hot and garnush with coriander.
