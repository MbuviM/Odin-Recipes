Chapati

An African cuisine that is loved by many due to its diversity. It is made out of wheat and can be served with any stew.

Ingredients

  • Wheat Flour
  • Hot water
  • 2-3 Onions
  • Salt
  • Cooking Oil
  • Sugar
  • Rolling Pin

Steps

  1. Add the warm water to a basin with wheat flour, salt and sugar.
  2. Knit the flour till it makes a dough.
  3. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Apply cooking oil to the dough.
  5. Cut it to sizeable pieces.
  6. Heat the pan and roll the small pieces using a rolling pin till they are circular.
  7. Cook the dough in the pan while flipping till it is perfectly cooked.
